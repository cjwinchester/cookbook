Steak marinade

dinner
marinade

Start to finish: 10 hours, 45 minutes

Based on a recipe on All Recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp. Sriracha sauce
  • Dash of peppercorns
  • 1 Tbsp. brown sugar

Instructions

Mix together ingredients in freezer bag. Add steaks to bag and shake well. Refrigerate for at least 10 hours, turning and shaking occasionally. (Usually I leave them in overnight.)

Remove steaks from bag 30 minutes for cooking and dab dry. Add a little salt and pepper to each side before grilling.
