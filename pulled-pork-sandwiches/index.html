Slow cooker pulled pork sandwiches

dinner
slow cooker

Start to finish: 9 hours

Goes well with spicy coleslaw.

Ingredients

  • 1 boneless pork shoulder roast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 can tomato soup

Instructions

Combine ingredients in a crock pot. Cook for 8-9 hours on low, stirring occasionally.

Remove pork from crock pot to cutting board and shred. Stir back into crock pot and let simmer on high heat for 15 minutes. Serve with toasted hamburger buns and vinegary coleslaw.
