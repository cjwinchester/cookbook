Lemonade pancakes

breakfast

Start to finish: 20 minutes

via

Ingredients

  • 2-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • 2 eggs
  • 2-1/2 scant cups milk
  • 2 Tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2-1/2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

Stir the lemon juice into the milk and let stand at least five minutes. Meanwhile, combine the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl and make a well in the center.

Beat the eggs into the milk mixture, then stir into the bowl of dry ingredients along with the melted butter. Whisk to combine. Add a little flour if the consistency is wrong. Heat a griddle and melt some butter or coconut oil on it. Drop 1/3-cup sized dollops of batter onto the griddle and cook until each side is finished (flip when you see bubbles).
