Pork chops like a boss

dinner

Start to finish: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 or 5 thick-cut pork chops
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
  • Bacon fat or olive oil
  • 1/2 sweet yellow onion, cut into thin strips
  • Garlic salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 425°. Thaw the chops. Beat the egg in a small bowl, adding a splash of milk and garlic salt and pepper to taste.

In a medium skillet (cast iron, if you have one), heat the bacon fat over medium-high heat. Once the pan is heated, dip the chops in the egg mixture, then coat in bread crumbs, then add to skillet. Add onions. Sear the chops until just charred on each side.

If your skillet isn't oven-proof, remove contents of skillet to oven-safe dish. Bake for 10 minutes, then flip. Bake for 10-15 minutes more, or until internal thermometer registers 160°.
