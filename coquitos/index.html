Coquitos

cocktail

Start to finish: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups white rum
  • 3.5 cups coconut milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk (14 oz)
  • 1 can creamed coconut (14 oz) -- Goya or similar, not e.g. Thai Kitchen
  • 3 Tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Pour all ingredients into a blender and liquefy.

Chill, shake well and serve over ice with a sprinkle of cinnamon and nutmeg on top.
