Spaghetti

dinner
pasta

Start to finish: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound hamburger
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper or jalapeño
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil or bacon fat
  • 1 pound wheat spaghetti
  • 1 Tbsp. brown sugar
  • 1 squirt Sriracha sauce

Instructions

Thaw hamburger. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large pan (high heat) and sauté the peppers, onions and garlic until slightly blackend. Remove vegetables to a bowl.

Reduce heat to medium and brown the meat in the pan. Drain off the fat. Reduce heat to low. Stir in spaghetti sauce, vegetables, brown sugar and Sriracha sauce. Let simmer, stirring occasionally.

In a pot, boil water with a pinch of garlic salt. Add spaghetti, cook until a noodle thrown against the wall sticks. Remove to large serving bowl.
