Pancakes

breakfast

Start to finish: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tablespoons baking powder
  • Dash of cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups milk
  • 1-1/2 Tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil

Instructions

In a large mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients. Make a well in the center. Melt the coconut oil and honey in a separate bowl. Beat the eggs into the milk and add dry ingredients, along with melted honey and oil. Stir until combined. Cook pancakes in a pan or on a griddle or whatever.
