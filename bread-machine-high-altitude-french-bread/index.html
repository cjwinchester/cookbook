Bread machine: High altitude French bread

bread

Start to finish: 4 hours

This recipe was made for a Welbilt bread machine, but I'm sure it'd be fine for whatever.

Ingredients

  • 1⁄2 Tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1⁄2 Tablespoon sugar or melted honey
  • 1⁄2 Tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1⁄2 Tablespoon softened butter
  • 1-1⁄2 cups water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Wheat flour for thickening, if necessary
  • A wee bit of melted butter, to taste

Instructions

Add the ingredients to the bread machine in this order: yeast, sugar/honey, garlic salt, butter, water, flour.

Set the machine to French Bread and hit start. Watch as the dough forms -- if it looks too sticky, add a little wheat flour.

Once it's done, drop the loaf onto a wire rack and spread some melted butter over the crust. Cool for 15 minutes before slicing.
