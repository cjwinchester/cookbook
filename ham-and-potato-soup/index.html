Slow cooker creamy ham and potato soup

slow cooker
soup

Start to finish: 8 hours, 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3-1/2 cups potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/3 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 jalapeño, diced (optional)
  • 1 cup cooked ham
  • 3-1/4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 5 Tablespoons butter
  • 5 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk

Instructions

In a small skillet, sauté onions and peppers in bacon fat until blackened.

Stir everything into the crockpot except milk, flour and butter. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.

About 20 minutes before serving, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Gradually add flour and milk, stirring constantly. Stir into crockpot and let cook for an additional 20 minutes before serving.
