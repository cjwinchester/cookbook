Strawberry rhubarb pie

dessert
pie

Start to finish: 1 hour, 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Pie crust with topper
  • 3 Tbsp. cornstarch
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
  • 3 cups chopped rhubarb
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 Tbsp. butter

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°.

In a large mixing bowl, combine cornstarch, sugar, salt and nutmeg. Add strawberries and rhubarb. Toss and let stand 15 minutes.

Pour mixture into pie plate and cover with crust topper. Bake for 1 hour. Remove and let cool for 15 minutes.
